A quick intro to tea definition & history.

Definition

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of Camellia sinensis. It’s the most widely consumed drink in the world after water, with flavors ranging from sweet and floral to grassy and roasted.

History

Tea spread across cultures for thousands of years. It originated in southwestern China and later became popular in Europe, especially Britain in the 17th century, shaping global tea trade and traditions.
